FAQ

Smart Waste Disposal for a Cleaner Future

What waste management services do you provide?
We offer residential, commercial, and industrial waste collection, recycling, hazardous waste disposal, dumpster rentals, and special waste removal.
How can I start service with your company?
You can sign up online, call our customer service, or visit our office to choose a plan that fits your needs.
What areas do you serve?
Our coverage varies by region. Please check our website or call us to confirm service availability in your area.
Do you provide same-day waste pickup?
Same-day pickup is available in select locations and for specific waste types. Contact us for details.
What items are accepted in regular waste collection?
General household and commercial waste, including non-recyclable materials. Hazardous waste and bulky items require special handling.
What are my collection days?
You can check your pickup schedule by entering your address on our website or calling customer support.
What should I do if my trash wasn’t collected?
Ensure bins were placed correctly, not blocked, and set out on time. If missed, report it online or by phone.
How do I dispose of bulky items like furniture or appliances?
Schedule a bulk waste pickup with us, or take them to a designated disposal site. Additional fees may apply.
What materials can be recycled?
Paper, cardboard, glass, aluminum, and certain plastics (check your local guidelines). Contaminated or mixed materials may not be accepted.
How should I prepare recyclables?
Rinse containers, flatten cardboard, and avoid placing non-recyclable items in the bin to prevent contamination.

We are committed to providing dependable, eco-friendly waste management solutions tailored to the unique needs of homes, businesses, and industries. By combining advanced technology with sustainable practices, we work to reduce environmental impact, increase efficiency, and support a cleaner, greener future for our communities and the planet.
